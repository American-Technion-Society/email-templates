This is an example headline

The Technion procedure, developed by Prof. Avi Schroeder and Dr. Assaf Zinger, softens the gum’s collagen fibers through the targeted release of the enzyme collagenase, allowing the teeth to shift into place. Braces would still be installed but for a far shorter time down from the conventional 2 years to about six months. The process is in preclinical trials and could be available within a couple of years.
